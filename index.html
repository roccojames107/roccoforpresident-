Who is Rocco?

About Rocco

My name is Rocco Pisani, and I'm running for School President because I want to make school better in ways students will actually notice every day. I'm someone who likes getting involved, meeting new people, and bringing energy into the school community — and I've been doing exactly that long before this campaign.

For over three years, I've been committed to Blood Cancer United, helping raise over $400,000 for blood cancer research and speaking at fundraising events to spread awareness and encourage donations. That work taught me something important: when you genuinely care about something and show up consistently, you can move people and make real things happen.

That's the same energy I'm bringing to Xaverian. I believe student government should be more than just a title — it should listen, take action, and keep working all year long. I'm ready to represent this school the same way I've represented a cause I believe in.

"Anyone can promise anything in a campaign — but the person you vote for should be the person you trust to actually get stuff done. This isn't just a title for us."

— Rocco Pisani, Candidate for President

The Plan

Platform

Here's exactly what Rocco, Peter, and Athena will fight for when elected.

01

Student Voice & Transparency

Monthly Google Forms so every student can request changes around school. Student polls for Spirit Week themes so your vote actually counts. Monthly "What We've Done This Month" posts so you can hold us accountable all year long — not just at election time.

02

Cafeteria Upgrades

Chipotle bowls more often — students love them and we're making it happen. Microwaves so you can heat up your food. Better coffee with different creamers and flavors. Improve breakfast with more options like protein smoothies and bars. Keep posting the cafeteria menu on Instagram. And open up the ice machine that's already in the building to students.

03

School Events & Spirit

Bigger, better sports events — show up to a game and get something for it. Buses to away games funded by bake sales. Senior events like Top Golf. A real club fair open to every grade, not just freshmen. Student polls for Spirit Week so the whole school has a say.

04

Smarter School Life

A bookable after-school study room for group studying and whiteboard access. Fix the AC in the chapel and cafeteria to a normal level. Improve the gym — broken equipment gets replaced. A segment on WXBN as student body president to keep the whole school informed. A health awareness week to encourage students to live better.

05

Community & Service

Weekend community service opportunities — beach cleanups, Blood Cancer United fundraising, and more — to help students earn hours and build their résumés. More service trips. A mascot tryout so Xaverian finally has a real Sailor to rally behind.
